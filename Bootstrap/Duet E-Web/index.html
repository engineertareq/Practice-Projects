About Duet

Welcome to the Website of Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology, Gazipur.
Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology, Gazipur (abbreviated as DUET) is a pioneer in engineering education in Bangladesh especially for the diploma engineers. DUET was founded as the College of Engineering, Dhaka in 1980. It was then renamed as Dhaka Engineering College (DEC) under the University of Dhaka in 1981, and used to offer Bachelor of Engineering
